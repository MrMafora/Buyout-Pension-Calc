# FedBuyOut Business - Operations Manual

## Business Model
**FedBuyOut.com** - Federal Employee Buyout Calculator
- **Revenue:** Lead generation for financial advisors serving federal employees
- **Leads captured:** Email subscribers, calculator users, consultation requests
- **Target buyers:** Fee-only financial planners, federal employee specialists
- **Value prop:** Pre-qualified federal employees actively considering buyouts

## Tech Stack
- Frontend: React + Vite
- Backend: Express + TypeScript
- Database: PostgreSQL
- Email: Resend API
- Hosting: DigitalOcean (this server)
- Domain: fedbuyout.com

## Lead Value
Each lead represents:
- Federal employee with buyout offer (high intent)
- Pension/calculation data (financial profile)
- Email verified (real person)
- Active decision-making mode (time-sensitive)

**Estimated value:** $50-200 per lead depending on service area & buyout size
