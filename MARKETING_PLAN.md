# FedBuyOut Marketing & Operations Plan

## Target Audience

### Primary: Federal Employees
- Age 45-60 (pre-retirement)
- FERS or CSRS retirement system
- Considering buyout offers (VSIP/VERA)
- Active decision-making mode

### Secondary: Financial Advisors
- Fee-only planners
- Federal employee specialists
- Looking for qualified leads

**Budget:** Start with $5-10/day, scale based on ROI
