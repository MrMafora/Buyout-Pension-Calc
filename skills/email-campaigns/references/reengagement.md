# Re-engagement Campaign Reference

Re-engagement sequences for cold leads who haven't opened emails in 30+ days.

---

## When to Use Re-engagement

**Trigger Conditions:**
- No email opens in 30+ days
- No clicks in 45+ days
- Multiple emails sent with zero engagement
- Lead added 60+ days ago with no activity

**Goal:** Win back engaged leads OR clean inactive emails from list

---

## Post-Re-engagement Actions

### If Lead Re-engages:
1. Tag as "Re-engaged"
2. Restart from appropriate sequence position
3. Reduce frequency temporarily (weekly max)
4. Monitor closely for next 30 days

### If Lead Doesn't Respond:
1. Add to suppression list
2. Archive (don't delete) lead record
3. Update campaign stats
4. Consider retargeting ads in 90 days

### If Lead Unsubscribes:
1. Honor immediately
2. Add to global suppression
3. Send confirmation: "You've been unsubscribed"
4. Keep record for compliance (CAN-SPAM)

## Best Practices

1. **Don't Wait Too Long** - 30-45 days is the sweet spot
2. **Be Honest** - Acknowledge the gap in communication
3. **Provide Value** - Even in re-engagement, educate first
4. **Make Unsubscribing Easy** - One-click, no confirmation
5. **Respect the Exit** - No "are you sure?" guilt trips
6. **Test Subject Lines** - Re-engagement is high-stakes
7. **Segment by Source** - Different approaches for different lead sources
8. **Time It Right** - Tuesday-Thursday, 9 AM local time
