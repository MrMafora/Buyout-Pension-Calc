# Content Calendar for FedBuyOut

**Recommended**: 2-3 posts per week

- 1 timely/news post (Wednesday)
- 1 deep-dive or case study (Friday)

## Content Pillars (Rotating Themes)

### Pillar 1: Education (40%)
- How-to guides
- Eligibility explanations
- Process walkthroughs
- FAQ posts

### Pillar 2: Decision Support (25%)
- Comparison posts
- Case studies
- Decision frameworks
- Assessment tools

### Pillar 3: Timely/News (20%)
- Policy updates
- Deadline alerts
- Breaking news
- Seasonal reminders

### Pillar 4: Community/Success (15%)
- Success stories
- Expert interviews
- Reader Q&A
- Behind-the-scenes

## Content Mix

| Content Type | Frequency | Examples |
|--------------|-----------|----------|
| Long-form guides (1500+ words) | 2/month | Complete VSIP guide, decision framework |
| Standard posts (800-1200 words) | 4/month | How-to articles, comparisons |
| Quick tips (500-800 words) | 4/month | FAQ answers, checklist posts |
| News/timely (600-1000 words) | As needed | Breaking news, deadline alerts |
| Case studies (1200+ words) | 1/month | Success stories, deep dives |
| Videos/infographics | 2/month | Process visuals, calculator demos |
