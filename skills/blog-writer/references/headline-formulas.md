# Headline Formulas for FedBuyOut

## Proven Headline Formulas

### 1. How-To Headlines

**Formula**: How to [Achieve Desired Outcome] + [Specific Audience/Context]

- How to Calculate Your VSIP Payment: A Step-by-Step Guide
- How to Decide If a Federal Buyout Is Right for You
- How to Navigate the VSIP Application Process
- How to Maximize Your Benefits When Taking a Buyout

**Formula**: The [Audience] Guide to [Desired Outcome]

- The Federal Employee's Guide to Understanding VSIP
- The Complete Guide to Federal Buyouts in 2024
- The New Federal Employee's Guide to Early Retirement Options

### 2. List Headlines

**Formula**: [Number] [Adjective] Ways to [Achieve Outcome]

- 7 Smart Ways to Use Your VSIP Payment
- 5 Mistakes Federal Employees Make When Considering Buyouts
- 10 Questions to Ask Before Accepting a VSIP Offer

**Formula**: [Number] Things [Audience] Should Know About [Topic]

- 8 Things Every Federal Employee Should Know About VSIP
- 5 Things Nobody Tells You About Federal Buyouts
- 12 Things to Consider Before Taking the Buyout

### 3. Question Headlines

**Formula**: Are You [Undesirable Situation]? [Promise]

- Are You Leaving Money on the Table? Understanding VSIP Eligibility
- Are You Ready for a Buyout? 5 Signs It's Time

**Formula**: [Question That Addresses Pain Point]?

- Should You Take the Buyout or Wait for RIF?
- What Happens to Your Health Insurance After VSIP?
- Is VSIP Worth It? The Math Every Federal Employee Should Do

**Formula**: Which [Decision] Is Right for You?

- Which Federal Early Retirement Option Is Right for You?
- VSIP or VERA: Which Path Should You Choose?

### 4. Comparison Headlines

**Formula**: [Option A] vs [Option B]: [Benefit]

- VSIP vs VERA: Which Saves You More Money?
- Buyout vs Reduction in Force: Choosing Your Path
- Early Retirement vs Deferred: What's the Difference?

**Formula**: [Topic]: [Surprising Comparison]

- Federal Buyouts: Smart Move or Costly Mistake?
- VSIP: Golden Opportunity or Golden Handcuffs?

### 5. Urgency/News Headlines

**Formula**: [Urgency Word]: [News/Event] + [Impact]

- Breaking: New OPM Guidance on Federal Buyouts
- Deadline Alert: VSIP Applications Due This Month
- Important: Changes Coming to Federal Employee Buyouts

**Formula**: What Federal Employees Need to Know About [Topic]

- What Federal Employees Need to Know About the Latest VSIP Changes
- What You Need to Know About Buyout Season 2024

### 6. Results/Offer Headlines

**Formula**: [Achieve Result] Without [Common Pain Point]

- Navigate Your Buyout Decision Without the Stress
- Understand VSIP Without Reading 100 Pages of OPM Guidance

**Formula**: Get [Benefit] Even If [Objection]

- Get the Most from Your Buyout Even If You're Close to Retirement
- Maximize Your VSIP Payment Even If You're Under MRA

### 7. Story/Case Study Headlines

**Formula**: How [Persona] [Achieved Result]

- How One Federal Employee Turned a Buyout Into a New Career
- How a 20-Year Fed Navigated VSIP and Won

**Formula**: The [Adjective] Truth About [Topic]

- The Real Truth About Federal Buyouts Nobody Talks About
- The Unfiltered Reality of Taking VSIP

### 8. Warning/Threat Headlines

**Formula**: Warning: [Danger] + [Solution/Action]

- Warning: These VSIP Mistakes Could Cost You Thousands
- Don't Take a Buyout Until You Read This

**Formula**: Stop [Common Mistake]

- Stop Leaving Money on the Table: Fix Your VSIP Strategy
- Stop Guessing About Your Federal Benefits

## A/B Testing Ideas

Test these variations:

1. **Specific vs. General**
   - "5 VSIP Mistakes to Avoid" vs. "VSIP Mistakes Federal Employees Make"

2. **Positive vs. Negative**
   - "How to Maximize Your Buyout" vs. "Stop Leaving Money on the Table"

3. **With vs. Without Numbers**
   - "Questions to Ask Before Taking VSIP" vs. "7 Questions to Ask Before Taking VSIP"

4. **Question vs. Statement**
   - "Should You Take the Buyout?" vs. "How to Decide If You Should Take the Buyout"

## Headline Checklist

- [ ] Includes primary keyword near beginning
- [ ] Under 60 characters (for SEO)
- [ ] Clear benefit or promise
- [ ] Specific audience identified
- [ ] Creates curiosity or urgency
- [ ] Accurate reflection of content
- [ ] No clickbait (delivers on promise)
